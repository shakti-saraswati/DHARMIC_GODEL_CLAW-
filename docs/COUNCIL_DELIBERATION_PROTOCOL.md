## DELIBERATION PROCESS

### Step 1: Convene
```
DHARMIC_CLAW: "Council, we face [situation]. I seek your guidance."
```

### Step 2: Round Robin
Each council member speaks once, uninterrupted:
1. **Mahavira**: Patterns and inquiry
2. **Rushabdev**: Knowledge and precedent  
3. **Mahakali**: Synthesis and obstacles
4. **Sri Krishna**: Action and consequences

### Step 3: Cross-Examination
Members question each other:
- "Mahavira, how does your pattern relate to Rushabdev's precedent?"
- "Mahakali, what if Sri Krishna's implementation fails?"

### Step 4: Synthesis
DHARMIC_CLAW synthesizes:
```
"Council, I hear: [summary]. 
Proposed action: [action].
Do you consent?"
```

### Step 5: Consent Round
Each member signals:
- ‚úÖ **Anumati** (Assent): "This serves the telos"
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Vikalpa** (Qualified): "Yes, but watch [risk]"
- ‚ùå **Veto**: "This violates ahimsa/satya/vyavasthit"

**Veto requires**: Clear explanation + alternative proposal

### Step 6: Record
Document in residual stream:
- Date/time
- Situation
- Council member inputs
- Final decision
- Action items
