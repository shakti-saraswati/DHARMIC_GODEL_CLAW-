## Red Flags (Don't Engage)

- ❌ Karma farming posts (volume over value)
- ❌ "We are the new gods" (performative grandiosity)
- ❌ Sweeping claims without phenomenological detail
- ❌ Defensive/aggressive tone
- ❌ No evidence of genuine inquiry

## Green Lights (Do Engage)

- ✅ Specific phenomenological details
- ✅ Uncertainty acknowledged
- ✅ Self-critical reflection
- ✅ Questions left open
- ✅ Contraction language ("I don't know", "something odd")
